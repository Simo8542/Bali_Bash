09:00 — ~11:30 · Villa Ampo

Villa Games

Four events at the villa. Points awarded per event. Black shorts on from the moment you wake up.

🕯️🔥
The Ultimate Matchstick Duel
Villa Event 1 · Head-to-Head · Best of 3
Villa Event 1
Stick It 🥬
The Ultimate Matchstick Duel · Head-to-head
  1. 15 matches laid on the table between two players.
  2. Players alternate turns. Each turn: remove 1, 2, or 3 matches — your choice.
  3. Win condition: The player left with the last match loses.
  4. Rock Paper Scissors determines who goes first.
  5. Best of 3 rounds decides the match winner.
Strategy: Plan ahead and control the match count. The optimal play is mathematical — if you can leave your opponent with a multiple of 4, they cannot win.
💥🔴
Navigate the Minefield
Villa Event 2 · Team Race · Villa Lawn
Villa Event 2
Minefield 💥
Secret route grid on the villa lawn · Teams race simultaneously
  1. A rope grid is laid on the grass. Each team has a secret safe route through it.
  2. Team members cross one at a time, stepping square to square, trying to find the path.
  3. Step on a mine = explosion. Penalty: spin 5 times + jumping jacks, OR 10 push-ups.
  4. After a mine hit, the next team member starts from the beginning.
  5. Watch the opposing team — you can learn their wrong squares from their mistakes.
  6. First team to get all members across wins the event.
Strategy: Cooperate and memorise the path. Coordinate team transitions for speed. Watch what the other team does.
🦆💦
Pool Rubber Duck Race
Villa Event 3 · 4 Rounds · No Hands
Villa Event 3
Pool Rubber Duck Race 🐼
4 rounds · End to end and back ×4 · No hands
  1. Each team gets a rubber duck at their end of the pool.
  2. Goal: move your duck from Start to the Other End and back 4 times.
  3. Every team member participates across the rounds.
Round 1Long straw — blow the duck. Aimed blowing beats powerful.
Round 2Forehead push only. No hands, no other forehead.
Round 3Deliberate waves only — coordinate for max speed.
Round 4Balloon air — inflate, release, direct. No hands on duck.
Strategy: Steady forehead contact beats jarring hits. Coordinate wave rhythm for Round 3.
🍬🍡
Malaco Caramel Race
Villa Event 4 · No Hands · Full String
Villa Event 4
Malaco Caramel Race 🍬
Speed eating · No hands · First to finish wins
  1. Each competitor receives a full string of Malaco caramel candy.
  2. The string hangs from your mouth only — dangling freely before the start signal.
  3. Eat the entire string as fast as possible. Chew, work, finish.
  4. No hands at any point. Any hand contact = immediate disqualification.
  5. First competitor to fully consume the string wins. Judge call is final.
Strategy: Suction and jaw speed over brute chomping. The string has to keep moving — once you stop pulling it in, you stall. Lateral jaw movement helps on the caramel sections.

Beach Games

Three events on the sand. Arrive ready. The relay does not wait for stragglers.

🏃‍♂️🌊🏃‍♂️
Canggu Relay Challenge
Beach Event 1 · 5 Stations · Fill the 8L Bottle
Beach Event 1 — Main Event
Canggu Relay Challenge 🏁
5 stations → fill the team 8L bottle · First full bottle wins
  1. Two teams. Each team runs the 5 stations in order, one member at a time.
  2. After completing all 5 stations, carry your 1L water bottle back to the start.
  3. Pour it into your team's 8L communal bottle.
  4. Next team member runs. Repeat until the 8L bottle is completely full.
  5. First team to fill their 8L bottle wins.
1Beach Tennis — 10 consecutive hits before moving on.
2Cornhole — score a bag in the hole before moving on.
3Dizzy Spins & Speed Ladder — spin 10 times, then run the ladder.
4Beer Chug — drink for time at the table.
5Ocean Water Run — fill 1L bottle in the sea, return to start.
Tip: Fastest legs on Station 5. Station 3 is where teams fall apart — practice your dizzy sprint.
⛳🍺
Giant Beach Beer Pong
Beach Event 2 · Giant Bins · Two Footballs
Beach Event 2
Giant Beach Beer Pong ⛰️
Giant trash bins · Two footballs · Classic rules, massive scale
  1. Bins arranged in beer pong formation. Two teams, two footballs.
  2. Both balls in the same bin = remove 3 bins.
  3. Two different bins hit = balls come back, no bins removed.
  4. Non-starting team always gets a redemption shot after a winning turn.
  5. Team to clear all opposing bins wins.
Tip: Coordinate throws so both land together. Split shots cancel out — you need the pair.
🏆🍺
Survivor Beer Bottle Balance
Beach Final · Last Man Standing · All Compete
🎉 Beach Final Event
Survivor Beer Bottle Balance 🍻
Last man standing · Full arm extended · All team members participate
  1. All competitors stand with a beer bottle balanced on their palm, arm fully extended.
  2. Goal: be the last man standing with arm extended and bottle upright.
  3. Drop your bottle = you are OUT and must drink it.
  4. Buy-in option: hold a bottle on each arm for double points — but if either drops, you're out and drink both.
  5. Winner takes maximum points. Positions determine team score.
🎉 Final event. The team standings at this point may determine everything. Coordinate with your team — sacrifice plays are valid.
