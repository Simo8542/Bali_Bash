💡 Local Tips

Best nights: Monday (Mexicola), Thursday (Desa Kitsune), Wednesday (Old Man's beer pong). Avoid Saturday for big group bookings — peak tourist crowds.
Group tabs: Many venues require minimum spend for group bookings. Group tabs are smoother than individual rounds.
Warungs: For casual lunches, local warungs are exceptional value. Nasi goreng under 50,000 IDR (~30 DKK). Don't skip them.

🛂 Visa — Do This Before You Fly

Indonesia requires a Visa on Arrival (VOA). Apply online in advance — skips the airport queue.

  1. Go to molina.imigrasi.go.id — Indonesia's official e-VOA portal
  2. Apply at least 3 days before travel. Cost: ~USD 35
  3. Upload passport photo page + recent headshot
  4. Pay by card. You'll receive an approval email — screenshot it
  5. At Ngurah Rai airport, use the e-VOA lane (faster than the regular VOA counter)
  6. Valid 30 days, extendable once for 30 more days at an immigration office

⚠️ Verify requirements for your specific passport at imigrasi.go.id
