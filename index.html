💡 Local Tips

Best nights to go out: Monday (Mexicola & Old Man's), Thursday (Desa Kitsune), Wednesday (Old Man's beer pong night). Avoid Saturday for big group bookings — peak tourist crowds.
Ordering tip: Many venues require minimum spend per person for group bookings. Always check before ordering rounds separately — group tabs are usually smoother.
Warungs: For casual lunches, the local warungs around Canggu are exceptional value. Nasi goreng and mie goreng for under 50,000 IDR (~30 DKK). Don't skip them.

Practical Info

🛂 Visa — Do This Before You Fly

Indonesia requires a Visa on Arrival (VOA) for most nationalities. You can get it at the airport OR apply online in advance. Online is strongly recommended — skips a long queue.

  1. Go to molina.imigrasi.go.id — Indonesia's official e-VOA site
  2. Apply at least 3 days before travel. Cost: ~USD 35
  3. Upload passport photo page + recent headshot
  4. Pay by card. You'll receive an approval email — print it or screenshot it
  5. At Ngurah Rai airport, go to the e-VOA lane (faster than the VOA counter)
  6. The visa is valid 30 days, extendable once for another 30 days at an immigration office

⚠️ Double-check requirements for your specific passport — Danish, Singaporean and Hong Kong passport holders all qualify but always verify at imigrasi.go.id
