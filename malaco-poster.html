VILLA GAMES

Malaco Caramel Race

No hands · Full string · First to finish wins · Villa Ampo, Bali
Official Rules
How to Play
  1. Each competitor receives a full string of Malaco caramel candy. Hold it at arm's length, ready to place in mouth on the start signal.
  2. On the whistle: place the candy string in your mouth so it hangs freely from your lips — dangling out, not bitten through.
  3. Eat the entire string as fast as possible. Chew, suction, work it in. The full string must be consumed.
  4. NO HANDS at any point. Any contact between hands and candy = immediate disqualification. Arms must stay at your sides or behind your back.
  5. First competitor to fully consume the string and open their mouth empty wins the duel. Referee decision is final.
  6. Series format: Head-to-head duels across team members. Most wins takes the event point for their team.
◈ Techniques & Strategy
  • Suction beats brute chomping — pull the string in rhythmically.
  • Lateral jaw movement helps break down caramel sections faster.
  • Once you stop pulling it in, you stall — keep the momentum going.
  • Breathe through your nose. Panicking slows the jaw.
  • Straight posture helps — leaning forward gives your jaw more purchase.
